# PART 2: ORLANDO (Jun 30-Jul 2)

## Overview
- **Duration:** 3 full days
- **Focus:** Disney World (3 major parks)
- **Parks:** Magic Kingdom, EPCOT, Animal Kingdom
- **Experience:** Theme park magic for entire family

## BEFORE YOU GO: Disney Strategy

### Tickets & Pricing
- **Park Hopper vs Single Park:**
  - Single park per day: ~$109-129/person/day
  - Park Hopper (visit 2+ parks/day): +$80-100/person for whole stay
  - **Recommendation:** Buy single-park tickets, focus 1 park/day

- **Where to buy:**
  - Official Disney site (disneyworld.disney.go.com)
  - Ticketing brokers (may save $5-10)
  - Buy in advance (prices vary by date)

- **For 4 people, 3 days:**
  - Single park: $1,308-1,548
  - Park Hopper: $1,488-1,728
  - **Recommendation:** Single park ($1,300-1,550)

### FastPass+ (Lightning Lane)
- **What it is:** Skip-the-line passes for attractions
- **Cost:** Included with ticket (2-3 per day) OR Individual Lightning Lane ($15-25/attraction)
- **Strategy:**
  - Book 3 popular attractions for morning
  - Use later for other attractions
  - Hit popular attractions early (before FastPass lines get long)

### Touring Strategies
- **Magic Kingdom:** Arrive EARLY (8:00 AM opening), hit Cinderella's Castle area first
- **EPCOT:** More spread out, less rushing, explore at own pace
- **Animal Kingdom:** Arrive early for Flight of Passage, hit Pandora first

### Dining Reservations
- Book 60 days in advance (opens online)
- Popular spots fill up quickly
- Make reservations via My Disney Experience app

### Magic Kingdom Strategy (11 AM - 9:30 PM)

**What to Know:**
- Park opens 9:00 AM (early entry for hotel guests 8:00 AM)
- Crowds peak 12-3 PM (lunch time)
- Magic decreases again 4-6 PM
- Returns strong 7-9:30 PM (dinner time)
- Fireworks: typically 9:00-9:15 PM

**Top Attractions (Don't Miss):**
- **Cinderella's Castle** (photogenic, not a ride)
- **Space Mountain** (indoor roller coaster, Tomorrowland)
- **Splash Mountain** (log flume, Frontierland) *[Note: closing/reimagining, check status]*
- **Haunted Mansion** (iconic, ghostly fun)
- **Pirates of the Caribbean** (classic dark ride)
- **It's a Small World** (iconic, fun for all ages)
- **Jungle Cruise** (silly live commentary, entertaining)
- **Hall of Presidents** (air-conditioned break, history)

**Recommended Touring Order:**
1. **Arrive 10:00 AM:** Get in line before rope drop
2. **11:00 AM:** Run to Space Mountain (Tomorrowland) - shortest line early
3. **12:00 PM:** Splash Mountain or Haunted Mansion
4. **1:00-2:30 PM:** Lunch break (hit food stands)
5. **2:30-4:00 PM:** Hit other attractions, explore, less crowded
6. **4:00-5:30 PM:** Return to hotel, rest (bypass 5-6 PM peak crowd time)
7. **5:30-7:00 PM:** Dinner (reservation if booked)
8. **7:00-9:30 PM:** Final attractions, fireworks

**Dining Recommendations:**
- **Character Dining:** Cinderella's Royal Table (inside castle, book ASAP), Chef Mickey's, Akershus
- **Quick Service:**
  - Pecos Bill (tacos, Frontierland)
  - Pinocchio Village House (Italian, Fantasyland)

### EPCOT Strategy

- Less intensive than Magic Kingdom (more walking, less frantic)
- Better for leisurely pace
- Good food options

**Top Attractions:**
- **Future World:**
  - Test Track (high-speed car ride, Tomorrowland vibe)
  - Soarin' (flying simulator, relaxing)
  - Mission: Space (space simulator, intense)
  - The Land Pavilion (aquaculture/farming exhibits)

- **World Showcase:**
  - Canada, UK, France, Morocco, Japan, America, Italy, Germany, China, Norway, Mexico
  - Each pavilion has restaurants, shops, entertainment
  - Watch cultural shows
  - Eat food from each country

**Recommended Order:**
1. **10:00 AM-12:00 PM:** Hit Future World attractions (Test Track, Soarin')
2. **12:00-1:30 PM:** Lunch (try international food)
3. **1:30-4:00 PM:** World Showcase exploration
   - Walk through pavilions
   - Watch shows (typically 15-20 min entertainment pieces)
   - Shop, explore
   - Ice cream/snacks
4. **4:00-5:30 PM:** Return to Future World, hit remaining attractions
5. **5:30-7:00 PM:** Dinner at World Showcase pavilion
6. **7:00-9:00 PM:** Fireworks + final walk

**Food Highlights:**
- **France Pavilion:** Crêpes, wine
- **Japan:** Sushi, ramen
- **Mexico:** Tacos, churros
- **Germany:** Sausages, pretzels
- **Italy:** Pasta, gelato
- **Character Dining:** Akershus (frozen-themed, Princess Aurora present)

**Estimated Cost:**
