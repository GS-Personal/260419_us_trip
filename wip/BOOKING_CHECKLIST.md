### ✅ **4-5 WEEKS BEFORE (May 17-24)**

**Priority: MEDIUM - Book dining, Amtrak, Broadway, NYC hotel**

- [ ] **Disney character dining reservations (60-day window opens May 29)**
  - Book EXACTLY 60 days before (May 29-30)
  - Recommended meals:
    - **Akershus Royal Banquet (EPCOT, Norwegian pavilion):** Character dining, lunch
    - **Cinderella's Royal Table (Magic Kingdom):** Princess breakfast
    - **Chef Mickey (Contemporary Resort):** Breakfast with Mickey
  - Link: [Disney Dining](https://www.disneyworld.disney.go.com/dining/) | Phone: 1-407-WDW-DINE (1-407-939-3463)
  - Cost: $60-75/person per meal × 2-3 meals = **$240-450**
  - **How to book:** Disney Dining opens reservations 60 days in advance. Set phone alarm for 12:01 AM ET on May 29 and call immediately (high demand, spots fill within minutes for summer peak).

- [ ] **Amtrak Northeast Regional (Boston↔NYC, Jul 10 & Jul 13)**
  - Window: 30-60 days before (May 19-June 3)
  - Route: Boston South Station ↔ Penn Station NYC
  - Schedule: Evening departure ~6-7 PM, arrive ~10:30 PM (overnight journey, scenic)
  - Cost: ~$50-80/person one-way × 3 passengers × 2 trips = **$300-480**
  - Link: [Amtrak.com](https://www.amtrak.com)
  - Booking strategy: Book 30+ days for discounted fares. Arrive at station 30 mins early.
  - **Tip:** Roomette upgrade ($100-150 additional) allows sleeping berth but may not fit budget; coach seating is adequate for overnight scenic ride.

- [ ] **Broadway show tickets**
  - Window: 4-6 weeks (May 17-June 3)
  - Recommended shows (family-friendly):
    - **Lion King:** Minskoff Theatre, $80-200/ticket
    - **Aladdin:** New Amsterdam Theatre, $80-180/ticket
    - **The Book of Mormon:** $100-250/ticket (more for older teens)
    - **Hadestown:** $90-200/ticket
  - Cost: 4 tickets × average $120 = **$480** (premium: $250-800)
  - Link: [Broadway.com](https://www.broadway.com) | [TKTS](https://www.tktimes.com) (day-of discounts, 20-30% off)
  - Booking: Official theater websites or [StubHub](https://www.stubhub.com) | [Ticketmaster](https://ticketmaster.com)
  - **Strategy:** If budget-conscious, buy day-of at TKTS Times Square booth (opens 10 AM, discounts to $80-120 for many shows). Book premium earlier if preferred seats matter.

- [ ] **NYC hotel (2 nights: Jul 12-14)**
  - Window: 5-6 weeks (May 17-24)
  - Location: Midtown or near Penn Station (convenient to Amtrak)
  - Options:
    - **Mid-range Midtown:** Hyatt Times Square, Marriott Marquis ($180-260/night) | [sites](https://www.hyatt.com)
    - **Budget Midtown:** Pod Hotel, Jane Hotel ($90-140/night) | [site](https://www.thepodhotel.com)
    - **Off-Midtown option:** Queens (LIC area), cheaper ($80-120/night), 15 min to Penn Station
  - Cost: 2 nights × $150 = **$300** (or $180-240 budget option)
  - **Amtrak alternative:** Take late-night Amtrak from Boston (depart 11 PM), arrive NYC 3-4 AM, nap at hotel. Return early morning train. Saves 1 night hotel cost (~$150) but loses sleep quality. **Not recommended.**
  - Booking: [Hotels.com](https://hotels.com) | [Booking.com](https://booking.com)

- [ ] **Statue of Liberty & Ellis Island tickets**
  - Window: 4-5 weeks (May 19-June 3)
  - Link: [StatueOfLibertyTours.com](https://www.statueoflibertytours.com) | Ticket booth at Battery Park
  - Options:
    - **Reserve + Pedestal access:** $24/adult, $18/child | ~$80 for family
    - **Crown access (summit):** $26/adult, $20/child | ~$92 for family
    - **Ferry only (island views):** $18/adult, $9/child | ~$54 for family
  - **Recommendation:** Reserve + Pedestal for good views without extreme crowds/height anxiety
  - Booking: Book online to skip ticket lines, guaranteed entry
